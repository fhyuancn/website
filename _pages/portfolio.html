My research advances an interdisciplinary understanding of water–carbon dynamics in agricultural and natural ecosystems under global change. I integrate field observations, manipulative experiments, and mechanistic modeling with emerging data-driven approaches within a Model–Experiment Integration (ModEx) framework to reduce uncertainties in predicting ecosystem responses.
