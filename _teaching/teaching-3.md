The Teaching in Globally Diverse Classes certificate program strengthened my ability to implement inclusive teaching strategies by incorporating multiple instructional formats—lectures, interactive discussions, group projects, and hands-on exercises—to accommodate diverse learning styles. After completing the program, I developed assignments that fostered collaboration among students from different disciplinary backgrounds, enabling them to learn from each other’s expertise. In computational courses, I provided scaffolded exercises and accessible coding resources, supporting students with varying levels of prior programming experience and enhancing overall engagement and learning outcomes.
