This certificate program strengthened my ability to implement inclusive teaching strategies by incorporating multiple instructional formats, including lectures, interactive discussions, group projects, and hands-on exercises, to accommodate diverse learning styles. I plan to design assignments that foster collaboration among students from different disciplinary backgrounds, enabling them to learn from each other’s expertise. In computational courses, I will provide scaffolded exercises and accessible coding resources to support students with varying levels of prior programming experience.
