I designed courses that turned complex concepts into hands-on, collaborative learning experiences. In my process-based crop modeling module, students from diverse backgrounds, from soil science to computuer science, explored how models represent real-world biological and environmental processes in agricultural systems. Through interactive discussions and group modeling exercises, they moved from theory to practice, gaining the skills to tackle real-world agricultural and environmental challenges.
